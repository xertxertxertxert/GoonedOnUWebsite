About Us

Welcome to Gooned On U Tag, a rising force in the Fortnite esports scene. Built from a shared passion for competition, creativity, and pure grind, our team is all about pushing the limits — both in-game and in the community.

At GOUT, we don’t just play Fortnite — we live it. Our roster is stacked with players who are fast, smart, and fearless, whether it's in solo clutch moments, intense scrims, or high-stakes tournament play. Every edit, every rotation, every outplay is about more than winning — it's about sending a message. When you see the tag, you know you’ve been outplayed by someone who puts the hours in and makes it look easy.

Our name might get a laugh, but make no mistake — we take our game seriously. We’re about discipline, development, and domination. We train daily, study the meta, build team chemistry, and focus on constant improvement. We believe that real success comes from combining natural talent with a relentless work ethic.

Gooned On U Tag isn’t just a team — it’s a mindset. A commitment to excellence, hype, and leaving a lasting impression in every lobby, tournament, and community we’re part of. Whether it's Cash Cups, FNCS, or showing out on stream, we represent the future of Fortnite competition.

You’re either with us, or you’re getting gooned on.
